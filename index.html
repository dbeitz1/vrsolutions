Services

K12 Safety and Security

For school districts that are looking to pair VR imaging with a county-wide safety plan.

Our school safety package includes complete coverage of your building. We scan every room, hallway, kitchen, bathroom and closet. It is the most comprehensive package we offer. We have the ability to label almost any item or location, such as safe rooms, exits, and priority areas. This package would ideally be paired with a school-based safety plan.

This package includes a floor plan view, a dollhouse view that can be manipulated to see different angles, and a 360 tour of the building.

School Promotion

Our school promotion package is custom made to fit your needs. We work with our clients to develop an individual plan to meet your goals. Pricing varies and is based on project needs.

Tips

Tips Before we Scan

Remember, how things look in real life is how they will look on your virtual tour. Make sure your place of business is how you want it before we come.

During the scan

Relax, this is our job. Scanning times depend on the layout of the building. Tight corners and long monotonous areas, such as hallways or open corridors, will require more time.

After the scan

We will edit the tour, add in any extras you would like and send you a preview of the product. Once everything is to your liking, we will send you the final copy and assist with any technical questions you may have.
